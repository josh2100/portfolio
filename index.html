About Me

Hey, I'm Joshua Schermann! I grew up in Pine River, MN, and moved to Minneapolis in 2010. I earned my Bachelor of Design in Architecture at UMN. I worked as a Pile Driver for the last 8 years and made site layouts, built bridges, buildings, foundations, and assembled crawler cranes.

I like snowboarding, travelling, hiking, camping, weightlifting, video games, and trading stock options.

My skills include HTML, CSS, Responsive Design, JavaScript, GitHub. I am currently learning MERN stack at University of Minnesota Coding Bootcamp.
