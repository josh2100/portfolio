About Me

Hello! My name is Joshua Schermann. I grew up on a dairy farm in Pine River, MN, and moved to Minneapolis in 2010. I earned my Bachelor of Design in Architecture at the University of Minnesota. For the last 8 years I worked as a Pile Driver and assembled crawler cranes, built bridges, buildings, and foundations.

Now I am learning MERN stack with the University of Minnesota Coding Bootcamp. I live in Coon Rapids, MN with my girlfriend. I like snowboarding travelling, hiking, camping, weightlifting, video games, and trading stock options.
